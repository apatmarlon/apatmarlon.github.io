About the ICT Department

The Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Department is dedicated to advancing digital transformation and innovation across public and private sectors. We develop technology-driven solutions that promote efficiency, transparency, and sustainable growth through the use of cutting-edge systems and secure digital infrastructures.

  • Empowering local communities through smart technologies
  • Promoting ICT-driven economic growth
